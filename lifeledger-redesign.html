The Problem

Most people have no idea what they own —
until it's gone.

Insurance companies pay based on what you can prove. After a fire or flood, most homeowners forget 30–50% of what they owned. That means a smaller check — sometimes thousands smaller.

🔥

House Fire

Without documentation, insurance adjusters rely on your memory under stress. A panicked, incomplete list means a smaller payout. Life Ledger keeps your proof safe in the cloud — untouched by flames.

🌊

Flood or Hurricane

When water destroys everything in minutes, serial numbers and purchase prices are the last thing on your mind. Have it documented before disaster hits.

🔓

Theft or Burglary

Police reports require specifics. Insurance claims require proof. Without a prior inventory, you're at the mercy of what you can remember — and what adjusters decide to believe.

📦

Moving

Things get lost, damaged, or "misplaced" during every move. An up-to-date inventory protects you from movers who conveniently forget what they broke.
